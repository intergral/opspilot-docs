# Invitations

View pending invitations and invite new members to your organisation. Navigate to **Organization > Invitations** to open it.

## Inviting a member

Click **+ Invite** to send an invitation. The invited user will receive an email with a link to join your organisation.

Pending invitations are listed here until accepted or cancelled. Once accepted, the user appears in [Members](users.md).
